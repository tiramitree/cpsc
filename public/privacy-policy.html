Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What We Collect

We may collect eBay user identifiers and listing information for the sole purpose of checking violations against recalled products.

How We Use the Data

The data is used only for internal analysis and reporting to the Consumer Product Safety Commission (CPSC). We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and use best practices to protect the data we store and process.

User Consent

By signing in via eBay, you consent to share limited account and listing information with our system for violation analysis purposes.

Contact

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at cpscgroup6@vt.edu.
